## How to Use

1. **Open the planner**: Open `index.html` in any web browser
2. **Browse activities**: Scroll down to see all 18 available activities with detailed information
3. **Filter activities**: Use the category buttons to filter by Resort, Beach, Adventure, Wildlife, etc.
4. **Build your itinerary**: Click "Add Activity" for any day to select activities from the full list
5. **Manage your plan**: Remove activities as needed by clicking the "Remove" button
6. **Save automatically**: Your itinerary is saved automatically in your browser
7. **Print**: Click "Print Itinerary" to create a printable version

## Tips for Planning

1. **Balance activity levels**: Mix adventure days with relaxing resort days
2. **Consider the young ones**: Most activities are suitable for ages 5+, some for all ages
3. **Book in advance**: Popular tours (catamaran, national parks) should be reserved ahead
4. **Weather**: June is green season (rainy season), usually sunny mornings with afternoon showers
5. **Transportation**: Many tours include pickup from the resort, or you can rent a car

## Cost Planning

- **Budget-Friendly**: Resort beaches, kids club, nature walks, waterfalls ($0-25/person)
- **Mid-Range**: Wildlife centers, national parks, beach day trips ($30-80/person)
- **Premium**: Guided tours, catamaran cruises, safari parks ($70-125/person)
- **Private Options**: Private catamaran ($870-1,200/boat), private beach setups ($100-200)
